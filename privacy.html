Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

The short version

Quiet Tarot keeps your data on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no interest in your personal information. If you have iCloud enabled, your readings and journal are automatically backed up to your personal iCloud account.

What data stays on your device

Everything you do in the app stays on your phone:

  • Your daily draws and spread readings
  • Your journal entries and reflections
  • Your weekly focus selections
  • Your display name and preferences
  • Your notification settings

This data is stored locally using your device's storage. Quiet Tarot never sees it and can't access it.

iCloud Backup

If iCloud is enabled on your device, Quiet Tarot automatically backs up your readings, journal entries, weekly focus, and preferences to your personal iCloud account using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage. This means your data is protected if you delete the app or switch to a new device.

This backup goes directly to your iCloud account. Quiet Tarot never sees, processes, or has access to your iCloud data. Apple manages the storage and encryption. You can disable iCloud for this app in your device's iCloud settings.

No additional sign-in, account creation, or permission is required.

What Quiet Tarot collects (if you opt in)

During setup, you can choose to share anonymous usage data. You can turn this off at any time in Settings → Privacy.

If you opt in, Quiet Tarot sends anonymous analytics to TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service based in the EU. Here is exactly what is collected:

Usage events (what you did, not what you wrote):

  • App opened
  • Tabs viewed
  • Cards drawn (which card and orientation, not your interpretation or reflection)
  • Spread readings started (type and cards drawn)
  • Journal entries completed (resonance level and focus categories selected, never your written reflections)
  • Weekly focus set (categories selected)
  • Subscription actions (tapped or completed)
  • Onboarding completed (which preferences were chosen)

Device context (sent with every event):

  • App version
  • Device platform (iOS) and OS version
  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 15)
  • Screen size
  • Light or dark mode

What is never collected:

  • Your name, email, or Apple ID
  • Your journal text or reflections
  • Notification settings or schedules
  • Your IP address (TelemetryDeck does not store IP addresses)
  • Any data that could identify you personally

All analytics are tied to a random anonymous ID generated on your device. This ID cannot be linked to you or your Apple account. TelemetryDeck's privacy policy is available at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

Subscriptions

Quiet Tarot+ is an optional annual subscription (€6.99/year) processed through Apple's App Store. Quiet Tarot uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status.

RevenueCat receives a transaction receipt from Apple to verify your subscription. This does not include your name, email, or Apple ID. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Quiet Tarot never sees or handles your payment information. All billing is managed by Apple.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification scheduling happens entirely on your device. Quiet Tarot does not use push notification servers.

Age rating

Quiet Tarot is designed for adults but contains no content unsuitable for children. Since no personal information is collected, there is no way to verify a user's age.
